Acceptance evidence is now separated into **logic verified**, **bridge verified**, **packaged interaction verified**, and **external acceptance**. Source-string contracts and browser mocks do not by themselves prove packaged macOS behavior. Ordinary mouse, keyboard, focus, fullscreen, title dragging, and window-resize checks are locally reproducible packaged-interaction work and are no longer classified as external. The mandatory matrix and the migration/acceptance failure analysis live in [`packaged-interaction-gate.md`](packaged-interaction-gate.md).
